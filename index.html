隐私政策

概述

HueMood 非常重视您的隐私。本政策说明我们如何收集、使用和保护您的信息。

我们收集的信息

您主动提供的信息

  • 您从相册上传的图片，仅在本地用于颜色提取，不会上传至任何服务器
  • 您创建的调色板数据，仅存储在您设备本地，不会上传

自动收集的信息

  • 订阅状态通过 Apple StoreKit 处理，我们不存储您的支付信息
  • Apple 可能根据其隐私政策收集崩溃报告等诊断数据

我们不做的事

  • 不收集您的姓名、邮箱或任何个人身份信息
  • 不追踪您的位置
  • 不将您的数据出售或分享给第三方
  • 不在服务器存储您的图片或调色板

第三方服务

本应用使用以下第三方服务：

  • Apple In-App Purchase（StoreKit）：处理订阅，适用 Apple 隐私政策
  • Unsplash：提供图片浏览功能，浏览时会向 Unsplash 服务器发出网络请求，适用 Unsplash 隐私政策

数据安全

所有用户数据均存储在您的设备本地，受 iOS 系统沙盒保护。

儿童隐私

本应用不面向 13 岁以下儿童，不会故意收集儿童个人信息。

联系我们

如有隐私相关问题，请联系：543407125@qq.com

政策变更

政策更新时，我们将在本页面更新日期。继续使用本应用即视为接受新政策。

Privacy Policy

Overview

HueMood respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Photos you upload are used locally for color extraction only and are never uploaded to any server
  • Color palettes you create are stored locally on your device only

Automatically Collected

  • Subscription status is processed via Apple StoreKit — we do not store payment information
  • Apple may collect diagnostic data per their own privacy policy

What We Don't Do

  • We do not collect your name, email, or any personally identifiable information
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties
  • We do not store your photos or palettes on any server

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple In-App Purchase (StoreKit): for subscriptions, subject to Apple's Privacy Policy
  • Unsplash: for image browsing; standard network requests are made to Unsplash's servers, subject to Unsplash's Privacy Policy

Data Security

All user data is stored locally on your device, protected by iOS sandboxing.

Children's Privacy

This App is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from them.

Contact Us

For privacy-related questions: 543407125@qq.com

Changes to This Policy

We will update the date on this page when changes are made. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
